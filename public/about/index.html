About Budibase

Budibase is a technology company with a belief that everyone should be able to build a SaaS application, run a SaaS business, and live life on their own terms. For the makers and entrepeneurs out there, Budibase is a platform of freedom and unlimited potential.

Where it started

Mike approached Joe with an idea to create a platform which would allow users to build business applications. After that initial coffee, the idea was flipped and they decided to go one step further and develop a SaaS ecosystem where users could easily build and manage SaaS applications. Budibase was found on the belief that everyone should have an opportunity to turn their SaaS dream into a reality.

Where it's going

We aim to continue to improve our platform and help our users develop SaaS applications, build businesses and change lives. We have high hopes for Budibase, and so do our users. We are creating an ecosystem which will drive SaaS development for many years to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize SaaS and transform dreams.

To democratise SaaS, we need a democratised ecosystem. And to have a democratised ecosystem, we open sourced Budibase. Creating a SaaS application should involve minimal setup, maximum security and be accessible by all. By breaking down these barriers, we are empowering focus. Focus to build a better product, business, and transform dreams into reality.
