What is Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing provides you with the opportunity to get digitally social with your prospects and customers. I can guarantee your customers and prospects are already interacting with brands through social media, and if you're not speaking to your audience through platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn, you're missing out! Smart marketing on social media can bring huge success to your business, generating leads, creating devoted brand advocates and even closing sales. Belfast social media marketing is hot right now, and you can't miss out.

Get Social_

The benefits of Social Media are plentiful, but this Belfast social media expert has picked 6 which will bring a smile to that beautiful face.


1. Brand Recognition

Brand recognition is a global business objective. Social media is a key platform for get your brand in from of customers quickly and easily.

2. Customer Engagement

Engaging with your customers on a regular basis is imperative. Customer churn can increase by 15% if you don't respond to customers on social media! (Gartner)

3. Website Traffic

Social media is a great platform for sharing your blog posts, promotions, or video content from your website. Who doesn't want more traffic to their website?

4. Targeted Advertising

Social media sites like Facebook have a lot of data about its users - sneeky buggers. As a result, their advertising platforms allow for very indepth audience targeting.

5. Social Sales

Sales teams are closing deals on social media by the minute. According to Hubspot, social media lead conversion rates are 13% higher than the average lead conversion rate.

6. Recruitment

Have a personality on social media can increase job applicants. Everyone wants to work for the cool company - social media is a great platform to show-off your personality.
