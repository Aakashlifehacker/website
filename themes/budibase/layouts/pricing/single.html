Pricing

Simple, honest, pricing

Building apps with Budibase is free - no CC required! When deploying your apps, you have two options; host your app with Budibase, or self-host. When hosting with Budibase, you can deploy straight from the builder and you will not pay anything until you pass the 1,000 record mark. After that, the fee is $20 per 2,000 records, including 2GB of attachment space.

Self-hosting

Self-hosting will require you to extract your app from Budibase, and host it wherever you please. If you decide to go down this route, will are happy to offer help when and where possible.
