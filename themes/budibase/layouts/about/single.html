About Budibase

Budibase is the amazing users, customers, and employees who come together to improve how the world develops SaaS apps. We'd be nothing without you. Thanks for everything.

What?

Budibase was founded in 2018 by Mike and myself (Joe). We setup the business in Belfast, but we're free to roam the world if we want to. We're a technology company with a strong belief in the 3 Budi P's; People, Product, Profit - in that order.

How?

Mike approached me with an idea to create a platform for building business apps. After that initial coffee, the idea was tweaked and we decided to develop a platform which would help users build SaaS applications at super-fast speeds. From the very beginning we agreed Budibase should be open source and bootstrapped. The future of Budibase will be decided by its users.

Why?

The purpose of building Budibase was to provide our users with the freedom to escape the 9-5 and live on their terms. We want to help makers, developers, and dreamers quickly turn their SaaS ideas into successful applications people love.

Our Mission

To turn dreamers into makers, and ideas into SaaS applications. Quickly.

Vision

To be the world's go-to place for creating SaaS applications.
